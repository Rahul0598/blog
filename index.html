Rahul Devajji

PhD Researcher in Informatics · Healthy Cities Lab · Indiana University Bloomington

I study urban heat — how cities concentrate it, who it harms most, and how we can use data to do something about it. My work combines satellite imagery, distributed sensor networks, and machine learning to model hyperlocal temperature and heat vulnerability across cities.

Research areas: urban climate · geospatial AI · earth observation · heat–health equity · spatiotemporal modeling
