Research

I am a PhD researcher in Informatics at Indiana University’s Healthy Cities Lab, advised by Dr. Dana Habeeb. I use geospatial AI and public-health data to map urban heat risk and build tools cities can use.

Current Work

  • Temperature prediction: hourly citywide air-temperature models from sparse sensors and AEF embeddings.
  • Local Climate Zones: LCZ classification in Google Earth Engine, validated against hyperlocal sensors. ArcGIS.
  • Heat-health analysis: models of heat-related ED visits using hospital, weather, and census data.
  • Heat planning tools: municipal HVI and sensor dashboards. HVI · sensors.
